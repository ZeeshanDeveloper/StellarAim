MuizUd Din Farid - Founder

Mr.MuizUd Din Farid, the founder and Board Member at Steller A.I.M has a propensity to push for purity and precision. Multi-talented and distinguished for his entrepreneurial ventures, he is equally as ambitious when spearheading projects as he is driven to delivering a novel experience to his clients.

Hailing from the culturally rich city of Lahore, he received his general education at Aitchison College Pakistan. He has also completed his BSC (Hons) in Applied Accounting from Oxford Brookes University, further enriching his knowledge with an ACCA qualification from Kaplan London UK.

With a penchant for multitasking, he is successively heading multiple ventures, which aptly reflect his passion for the culinary arts, hospitality, technology and construction. Credited with the conception of Baranh, Pakistan’s original heritage-revival concept-based restaurant as well as pioneering online construction portal called Construcit, he has amassed a building pool of growing brands.

He also serves as the Managing Director of Grand Ittehad Boutique Hotel in Lahore, his recently acquired hospitality venture. Over the years since his involvement in his family construction business Mark-Inn, the brand’s prominence has only soared.

His natural flair for real estate, familiarity with heading property development projects and an inclination towards the finer things in life naturally progressed into the birth of STELLAR A.I.M. His intention is to launch Pakistan’s premium luxury real estate agency that offers groundbreaking experiences, indulgent services and an access to luxury living unheard of in the local marketplace.

Asma Waqar - Director

Asma Waqar, a fervent supporter of women in the workforce and a prominent corporate woman herself is a Kinnaird College graduate. Recognized by various publications for her contributions to the hoteling and culinary industry, she ventures into the world of luxury real estate with STELLAR A.I.M.

An innovative and passionate entrepreneur, her expertise lies in creative ideation, marketing and brand development. She is also the face and founder of ‘Crave the Caterers’, a successful catering company that shines at the heart of every successful event in the city.

Possessing a keen eye for aesthetics, and equipped with a stellar skill set in selling and promotion, her function within the agency supports the buyer’s and seller’s market. She possesses a preference for perfection and loves doing things exceptionally yet differently.

Her enterprising style not only compliments the vision of STELLAR A.I.M but also generates impeccable results for the agency and the clients.

The thrill of continuous professional evolution drives her world, and as a member of the Board of Directors, she brings a contagious buzz of energy and excitement to STELLAR A.I.M.

Isbah Hassan & Associates - Director

Isbah Hassan has been a long-standing force to reckon with in the arena of architecture and design in Pakistan. She boasts over two decades of experience in delivering ingenious ideas that are aesthetically unparalleled, are mindful of the clients’ needs yet work in harmony with natural landscapes.

An alumnus of Illinois Institute of Technology (in Architecture) and The Art Institute of Chicago, she stepped into the first chapter of her career working for Fujikawa & Johnson in Chicago. She later relocated to Pakistan with an abundance of critical knowledge, working for Wasif Ali & Associates and Arshad & Shahid Abdullah Architects until finally launching her own Business.

She has since steadily risen to the status of an icon while manifesting her unique vision for exceptional constructions and design into reality. In what Isbah calls functional and responsive elegance, her creations lie on the belief of fostering a strong informative collaboration between the clients and the specialists on her team.

Emanating a strong sense of wisdom that comes with a diverse professional portfolio and the tenacity to accomplish innovative architecture, it is no wonder why her work overshadows that of her peers exponentially.

Credited with iconic projects across various sectors of Pakistan, her notably portfolio includes stellar commercial projects for United Bank Ltd, the urban planning projects for Bahria Paradise Rawalpindi and residential projects for her distinguished clientele.

At STELLAR A.I.M, she brings her expertise and an acute vision to bring forth a blend of smart functionality, and luxury aesthetics through carefully constructed interior design and architectural offerings. She brings a promise of lavish living solutions that are worthy of our clients’ preference for an indulgent and extravagant lifestyle.
